How Can The Negotiator Help Me?

The Negotiator knows what you want. To pay less for your house or car, or to claim as much as possible from a complaint. And he can help.

Vastly experienced, The Negotiator has his own businesses but saw an opportunity to use his skills to help others (and to make money himself!).

It's very simple. For example let's say you want to buy a house at £200k. If The Negotiator can agree the sale at £190k you split the difference 50/50. In this case you save £5k and The Negotiator earns a £5k fee. The better deal he can get the more you save and the more he makes, so there is always an incentive for him to drive the best possible deal.

If he cannot secure a saving there is nothing to pay. No win. No fee. No risk.

The Negotiator has done deals on residential and commercial property, vehicles, claims and much more. He's in demand and can't take on every enquiry so to find out if he can help you contact him today.
